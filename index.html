Privacy Policy for Milk Puzzle

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Milk Puzzle ("we") collect, uses, and protects user data. By using our game (com.adel.milk_puzzle), you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Milk Puzzle uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. The AdMob SDK automatically collects:

  • IP Address – Used to estimate general location
  • Device and Account Identifiers – Android Advertising ID (AAID) and app set ID for ad targeting and analytics
  • User Product Interactions – App launches, taps, and video views
  • Diagnostic Information – App performance data, crash logs, and energy usage

This data is collected for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes [citation:11].

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To improve game performance and user experience
  • To prevent fraud and ensure service security

3. Data Sharing

We share data with Google AdMob for advertising purposes. All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security) [citation:11].

4. Your Choices

You can reset or limit the use of your Android Advertising ID at any time:

  • Go to Settings → Google → Ads on your Android device
  • Select "Reset advertising ID" or "Opt out of interest-based ads"

5. Data Security

All user data collected by the AdMob SDK is encrypted during transmission. We do not store any personal data on our own servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Milk Puzzle does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
